Mandana Art

Rajasthan

Mandana art is a traditional folk painting style from Rajasthan, India, known for its symmetrical geometric patterns inspired by nature. Created with natural materials, it carries spiritual significance and is often used for festivals and rituals. Mostly practiced by rural women, it's now evolving and gaining wider recognition.

Warli Art

Maharashtra

Warli art is a traditional tribal art form originating from the Warli tribe in Maharashtra, India. It's characterized by its simple, symbolic, and geometric style. Created with basic shapes like circles and triangles, it depicts scenes from daily tribal life, nature, and rituals. Originally painted on walls, it has gained global recognition on various mediums. Warli art embodies the cultural heritage and community values of the Warli people.
